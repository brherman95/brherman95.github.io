Target Audience:

My Target Audience will be individuals seeking accurate daily weather forecast and conditons. These individuals are living or visiting the southeast region of the state of Idaho. They will come from all age groups, genders, and walks of life.

Persona 1

Anna Mathison is a Utah State College student heading to the Idaho side of bear lake for the weekend. She's studying to be a teacher and is 23 years old. She want's to bring some friends and enjoy time relaxing at the beach. Using Accuweather Idaho on her phone, she will quickly search conditions near the Idaho side of the lake.

Persona 2

Susan Stevensen is a local resident of Soda Springs planning an outdoor block party for the weekend. She will use the AccuWeather Idaho site on her tablet and computer to research weather conditions and forecast for the weekend to ensure they can still host the party outside. She is 56 years old.

Scenarios

  1. I go on a daily morning walk and need to check the weather conditions.
  2. I'm an elementary school principle and need to check the weather conditions for recess.
  3. I'm planning a family reunion at bear lake and need to see if weather will permit.
  4. My friend wants to go on a bike ride this weekend and I want to see the weather to see how to dress.
  5. I'm a farmer keeping up on weather conditions for my crops.
